Instructions!

Your goal is to keep Baby Groot alive and happy until he is old enough to leave his plant pot.

  • Grow - increases Baby Groots ages by 1.
  • Walking - improves Baby Groots fitness.
  • Feeding - reduces Baby Groots hunger.

A few tips and things to remember:

  • If Baby Groots fitness drops below 0 he will die!
  • If Baby Groots hunger is over 10 he will die!
  • If you can keep Baby Groot alive for 30 days he will survive and you will win the game
  • Baby Groot will tell you how he is feeling
